Story

Super Mario Bros. 35 combines run-and-jump platforming with battle royale elements. Thirty-five players each control the protagonist Mario, with Luigi being unlockable as a playable character through an Easter egg,[1] and simultaneously traverse a looped set of thirty-two two-dimensional levels.[2][3] These courses contain items, such as coins and power-ups, and enemies that are to be defeated.[4] A player wins once all other players are beaten.

Characters

You can play Mario and Luigi. The clones of Mario are only available in the GBA version.

Target and other enemies are Shellcreeper, Sidestepper, Fighter Fly, Slipice, Icicle, Fireball, Koopa Troopa and Bowser (Only in GBA version).
